Discovery Medmergency

The Discovery Medical Emergency (Medmergency) application is designed to make it easier for people needing medical attention to do so easily without always having to get a COVID-19 test each and everytime. The application also makes booking specialist doctors much easier since the doctor has the patients records and knows how critical their condition may be. It also allows emergency walk-ins with the team of standby medical emergency practitioners.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will cost me?

This is a pay per use service. The web application is available to Discovery Health and Vitality customers and use is billed to their accounts.

What do I do in an emergency?

In an emergency, dial the number on our homepage with your membership number for Discovery Health or Vitality.

What if I have an emergency and Im not registered.

Use the emergency form on the homepage to explain your situation and you will receive a call immediately
