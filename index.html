The Best
Medical Questionaire

Discovery's MedMergency offers the best medical web application in 16 countries globally. It is linked to your Discovery Health App and allows you to find a medical practitioner in your area and based on the results of your test, sets you as the practitioner's next priority.

The Best
Specialist Finder

MedMergency offers you the best General or Specialist practitioner search in your area. It links with over 1000 practitioners registered with Discovery Health to find one that is closest to you and one that is more readily available.

The Best
Self Diagnosis

With additional accessories, such as the smart watch or fit bit, the application is able to record your temperature, blood pressure, cough intensity and the data obtained is used to supplement your questionaire results and help get you the medical attention you require as fast as possible.
